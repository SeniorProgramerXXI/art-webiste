Artist Biography

Pusha T

Terrence Thornton, is an American rapper and songwriter known for his sharp lyricism and vivid storytelling. He gained prominence as one-half of the rap duo Clipse and later pursued a successful solo career.

Takashi Murakami

Takashi Murakami is a renowned Japanese contemporary artist known for his colorful and whimsical artwork that blends traditional Japanese art with pop culture elements. He's famous for his superflat style, which features bright colors, repetition of motifs, and a mix of high and low art influences.

The Weekend

Canadian artist, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He's known for his unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music. The Weeknd gained widespread recognition for his distinct voice and emotionally charged lyrics, with hits like "Blinding Lights" and "Can't Feel My Face."

Travis Scott

Jacques Webster II, is an American artist, rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He's known for his distinctive blend of hip-hop and trap music, characterized by his energetic performances and innovative production having high impact on the hip-hop producer.

Van Gogh

Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his expressive and vibrant paintings, including iconic works like "Starry Night" and "Sunflowers." He is celebrated for his unique artistic style, which featured bold colors, dramatic brushwork, and emotional intensity.

Kanye West

Kanye West is an American artist, rapper, songwriter, record producer, and fashion designer. He is a highly influential and controversial figure in the music industry, known for his innovative music, outspoken personality, and impact on fashion. His work has had a significant cultural and artistic influence.

Davinci

Leonardo da Vinci, one of the most renowned figures in human history. Leonardo da Vinci was an Italian Renaissance polymath who excelled in various fields, including painting (e.g., the "Mona Lisa" and "The Last Supper"), science, engineering, anatomy, and many others.

Frida Kahlo

Frida Kahlo was a Mexican painter known for her unique and colorful self-portraits that often explored themes of pain, suffering, and identity. She is celebrated for her contribution to modern art and her representation of Mexican culture.

Drake

Drake, whose real name is Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and businessman. He is one of the most successful and influential artists in contemporary music, known for his versatile style that blends hip-hop and R&B.

Michelangelo

Michelangelo, was an Italian Renaissance artist and one of the most renowned figures in the history of art. He excelled in various artistic disciplines, including sculpture, painting, and architecture. Michelangelo's masterpieces include the sculptural works "David" and "The Pieta".
